Vex Anarchy

From the newly created anarchy server rose two people creating the greatness now known as XS. Leo and Ness both founded Vex Anarchy; a colossal success of an anarchy server. While keeping admin abuse to it's minimums, Vex Anarchy ran for around 21/2 years. With over 80 unique joins, it's safe to say with the wars and history build within the server by its community, the server was a success.
Vex Anarchy was later brought down in early 2020 due to a new server being ran (see below) that would shift priorities leaving Vex to dwindle.
