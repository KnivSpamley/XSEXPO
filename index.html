XS-2019 Updates

Our Declaration

We, the XS Team, solemnly pledge that our operating servers shall remain bastions of fairness, equality, and respect for all players. No administrator, staff member, or role with greater authority than a new player shall engage in or permit actions that exploit their position. Misconduct, plugin exploitation, or any form of manipulation designed to grant unfair advantages—whether for personal gain or on behalf of others—stands strictly prohibited.
We commit ourselves to uphold the principles of integrity, ensuring that every player experiences a just and honest environment inherited by the council. Together, we will champion a community where fairness prevails, and the spirit of competition thrives.

Our Team

With the XS team's actual player count being privately selected, a large team isn't a necessity to running the server. Administrator power is spread among few individuals, and our key priorities include server optimization, longevity, and playerbase health.

Previous Servers

Vex Anarchy

From the newly created anarchy server rose two people creating the greatness now known as XS. Leo and Knivier both founded Vex Anarchy; a colossal success of an anarchy server. While keeping admin abuse to its minimums, Vex Anarchy ran for around 2½ years with over 80 unique joins.

XSCeon-2019

With a huge degree of success and an experiment gone right, the two team members expanded and created a newer server. This server would be the best server ran by XS called XS19. With a privately invited selection of players, lore, wars, XS19 is a social success with more than 30 players and 7 veterans.

Server Stats
